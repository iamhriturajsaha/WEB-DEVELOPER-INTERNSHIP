The Foundation

Founded in 2020 by Govind Shukla in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, the InAmigos Foundation is driven by a singular vision: collective action for grassroots empowerment.

We believe in the immense power of empathy to bridge societal gaps. From ensuring food security and digital literacy to women's empowerment, animal welfare, and massive tree-planting drives, InAmigos represents positive, transparent, and lasting change.
