Project JEEV

Compassionate animal welfare initiative rescuing, protecting, and feeding stray animals.

The Mission

Project JEEV is born out of deep empathy for the voiceless. We operate feeding drives for strays, facilitate rescues and medical treatments for injured animals, and run adoption campaigns to find forever homes for our furry friends.
