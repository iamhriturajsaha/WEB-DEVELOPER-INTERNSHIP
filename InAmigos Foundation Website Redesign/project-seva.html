Project SEVA

Distribution of essential food and clothing to underprivileged individuals and daily wage workers.

The Mission

Project SEVA is our flagship initiative to eradicate hunger at the grassroots level. Every day, countless individuals go to sleep hungry. SEVA is our commitment to ensure that no one in our community is deprived of a basic, nutritious meal and essential clothing.
